About Bethel Music Academy

Bethel Music Academy is a professional music academy dedicated to inspiring students through quality music education.

We offer Keyboard, Guitar, Vocal and Drums classes for children, youth and adults in both Online and Offline mode.

Our mission is to help every student Learn, Practice and Perform with confidence.
